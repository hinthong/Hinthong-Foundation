About Us

Hinthong Foundation is a Section 8 non-profit organization incorporated on 9 March 2026, working towards environmental conservation, community development, and cultural preservation.

Our Mission

To conserve biodiversity, empower communities, and promote sustainable livelihoods through community-led initiatives.

Our Focus Areas

  • Environmental & Biodiversity Conservation
  • Wildlife Protection & Rescue
  • Climate Action & Resilience
  • Community Livelihoods
  • Cultural Preservation
