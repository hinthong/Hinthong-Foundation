About Us

Hinthong Foundation is a non-profit organization registered under Section 8 of the Companies Act, 2013 on 9 March 2026. It is based in Tirap district of Arunachal Pradesh. The organisation works towards biodiversity conservation, environmental protection, cultural preservation, livelihood and community development.

Our Mission

To conserve biodiversity, empower communities, and promote sustainable livelihoods through community-led initiatives.

Our Focus Areas

  • Biodiversity Conservation
  • Wildlife Rescue
  • Environmental Protection
  • Climate Action & Resilience
  • Sustainable Livelihoods
  • Community Development
  • Cultural Preservation

Founding Members
