Our Projects

Biodiversity Documentation of Tirap

The project was on visual documentation of the biodiversity of Tirap district, Arunachal Pradesh. It was funded by the Green Hub X Royal Enfield Conservation Grant in 2022. The project culminated into a pictorial book titled 'Unexplored Tirap: A Bio-Cultural Portrait'. It highlights not only the variety of species found in the region but also the culture and traaditions of the different tribes inhibiting the district. Illustrated forms of folkstories was also included in the book.

Green Tirap Project

The project focuses on raising awareness on biodiversity conservation in New Tupi village, Tirap district in Arunachal Pradesh.
